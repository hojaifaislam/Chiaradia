Get inspired by our work

Puncture the tradeshow bubble

People like to be in spaces that feel human. We at Chiaradia Exhibitions design, create and build spaces people can sink into and relax. We use innovative materials and create quiet, private havens in the notoriously overwhelming environment of a tradeshow. Tradeshows are the place to be for innovative brands that are aiming to transform the world as we know it.

We at Chiaradia Exhibitions believe that these brands deserve nothing less than the best and most outstanding experiences to fully match their identity. We, together with the client, aim to innovate continuously. Year-on-year. For this purpose we have included some visual case studies that show how we make it happen.
